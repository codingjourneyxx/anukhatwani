Brand Story

A & MORE

FURNITURE STUDIO

A & MORE is the creative extension of Anu Khatwani's design philosophy where craftsmanship, culture, and contemporary living intersect.

At A & MORE, furniture is where heritage craftsmanship meets contemporary design. Rooted in time-honoured techniques and reimagined through bold, art-inspired forms, each piece is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans using solid wood, refined finishes, and thoughtful detailing.

Inspired by antique motifs yet shaped for modern living, our collections transform furniture into sculptural statements—where beauty, function, and storytelling coexist. Designed to elevate spaces, every creation reflects a balance of soul, structure, and innovation.

A & MORE is more than a design studio—it is a philosophy. A celebration of heritage and innovation, where every creation reflects refined taste, thoughtful craftsmanship, and soulful living.

" Blending global design with Indian heritage "

About

Anu Khatwani

Founder & Creative Director

Anu Khatwani is a visionary entrepreneur and interior designer, Founder and creative force behind A & MORE—a distinguished design studio known for its fusion of classical and contemporary furniture, handcrafted décor, and art-led interiors.

Her work seamlessly blends global design sensibilities with the richness of Indian heritage, resulting in creations that are timeless, sculptural, and deeply expressive.

With a strong academic foundation, she earned a degree in Interior Design from Parsons School of Design, New York, and graduated first-class in Business Management. This rare combination of artistic excellence and strategic acumen allows her to approach design with both creative depth and business precision.

Beyond her creative pursuits, she is a dedicated social worker, driven by a desire to give back and create meaningful impact.

What we create

Working across disciplines of Furniture, Interior Design, and Decor, the dedicated team at the design house, led by the immaculately polished style sense of Anu Khatwani herself, conceive and execute synergistic, forward-thinking and visually striking projects.

Furniture

Her collections embody a profound commitment to craftsmanship, innovation, and cultural narrative. Bold yet inviting, timeless yet contemporary, her designs are distinguished by an effortless elegance and organic richness. Spanning solid teak wood couches, hand-painted reproduction antiques, mirrors, lamps, and children's furniture, each creation is a sculptural dialogue of texture, finish, and form—bridging heritage with modern sensibilities.

Services

A leading designer of ultra-luxury estate homes

FURNITURE

Commissioning everything from concept, design and build, the House of Anu Khatwani creates luxuriously unique furniture that echoes the overall architecture and aesthetic palette of high-caliber spaces.

  • Seating — Handcrafted chairs, sofas, and lounges designed for comfort and elegance
  • Tables & Surfaces — Bespoke tables and consoles crafted with artisan detailing
  • Storage Pieces — Functional cabinets, chests, and wardrobes with timeless appeal
  • Dining — Elegant dining tables and chairs for memorable gatherings
  • Bedroom — Luxurious beds and nightstands for restful retreats
  • Custom Creations — One-of-a-kind pieces tailored to your unique vision

INTERIOR DESIGN

Discerning spaces deserve bespoke interiors. Anu Khatwani combines visual dexterity with an unparalleled access to internationally renowned materials, craftsmanship and processes that result in striking and exceptional homes.

  • Residential — Bespoke home interiors that reflect your personal style
  • Commercial — Sophisticated spaces for offices, retail, and hospitality
  • Custom Projects — Tailored design solutions for unique requirements

DÉCOR

From accents to accessories, the finishing touches highlight the soul of a space. We have an exclusive team of consultants who can help with commissioning or sourcing bespoke works including crystal chandeliers, precious display items, antiques and limited edition items.

  • Accent & Statement Pieces — Curated décor elements that elevate any space
  • Signature Pieces — Exclusive art-inspired creations with distinctive character

Services

Bespoke Furniture, Interior Design & Décor Solutions

FURNITURE

Commissioning everything from concept, design and build, the House of Anu Khatwani creates luxuriously unique furniture that echoes the overall architecture and aesthetic palette of high-caliber spaces.

Seating

Handcrafted chairs, sofas, and lounges designed for comfort and elegance. Each piece is meticulously crafted to provide both aesthetic appeal and ergonomic support, transforming any space into a haven of relaxation.

Tables & Surfaces

Bespoke tables and consoles crafted with artisan detailing. From statement coffee tables to elegant console designs, each surface piece becomes a functional work of art that anchors your living spaces.

Storage Pieces

Functional cabinets, chests, and wardrobes with timeless appeal. Our storage solutions seamlessly blend practicality with refined aesthetics, ensuring your belongings are housed in pieces worthy of display.

Dining

Elegant dining tables and chairs for memorable gatherings. Create the perfect setting for intimate dinners or grand celebrations with furniture that elevates every meal into an experience.

Bedroom

Luxurious beds and nightstands for restful retreats. Transform your private sanctuary with furniture designed to cocoon you in comfort while making a sophisticated style statement.

Custom Creations

One-of-a-kind pieces tailored to your unique vision. From conceptualization to final execution, we bring your dream furniture to life with uncompromising attention to detail and craftsmanship.

INTERIOR DESIGN

Discerning spaces deserve bespoke interiors. Anu Khatwani combines visual dexterity with an unparalleled access to internationally renowned materials, craftsmanship and processes that result in striking and exceptional homes.

Residential

Bespoke home interiors that reflect your personal style. We create living environments that are as unique as their inhabitants, blending functionality with artistic expression to craft homes that truly resonate with your lifestyle.

Commercial

Sophisticated spaces for offices, retail, and hospitality. We design commercial environments that enhance productivity, inspire creativity, and leave lasting impressions on clients and customers alike.

Custom Projects

Tailored design solutions for unique requirements. Whether it's a specialized space or an unconventional vision, we embrace challenges that push creative boundaries and deliver extraordinary results.

DÉCOR

From accents to accessories, the finishing touches highlight the soul of a space. We have an exclusive team of consultants who can help with commissioning or sourcing bespoke works including crystal chandeliers, precious display items, antiques and limited edition items.

Accent & Statement Pieces

Curated décor elements that elevate any space. These carefully selected pieces serve as focal points, adding personality and visual interest while tying together the overall design narrative of your interiors.

Signature Pieces

Exclusive art-inspired creations with distinctive character. These unique, often one-of-a-kind pieces become the defining elements of a space, showcasing exceptional artistry and becoming treasured heirlooms.

Anu Khatwani

Anu Khatwani is a visionary entrepreneur and interior designer

Founder and creative force behind A & MORE a distinguished design studio known for its fusion of classical and contemporary furniture, handcrafted décor, and art-led interiors. Her work seamlessly blends global design sensibilities with the richness of Indian heritage, resulting in creations that are timeless, sculptural, and deeply expressive.

With a strong academic foundation, earned a degree in Interior Design from Parsons School of Design, New York, and graduated first-class in Business Management. This rare combination of artistic excellence and strategic acumen allows her to approach design with both creative depth and business precision.

Anu's work showcased in leading fashion and lifestyle publications. Through curated previews and exclusive interior events, she brings emerging design trends to industry professionals and design connoisseurs alike. She is also actively developing a personal label aimed at taking Indian fusion furniture to a global audience.

Beyond her creative pursuits, she is a dedicated social worker, driven by a desire to give back and create meaningful impact.
